Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?

Simply browse our barber specialists, select your preferred master, and click "Book Now". You'll be guided to choose a date and time. You can also call us directly.

Can I choose a specific barber?

Absolutely! Every barber has their own profile with reviews and specialties. Pick the one that suits your style.

What services do you offer?

We specialize in fades, beard trims, kids' cuts, women's styling, and luxury hot towel shaves. All services are listed on each barber's page.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

No deposit is required, but we appreciate a 24-hour cancellation notice to avoid a small fee.

What if I'm late?

We allow a 10-minute grace period. After that, we may need to reschedule to respect other clients.

Are walk-ins accepted?

Yes, but priority is given to booked appointments. Walk-ins may have to wait.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Gift cards are available in any amount. Ask at the front desk or purchase online.

What safety measures do you have?

We follow strict hygiene protocols: sterilized tools, fresh towels per client, and regular sanitation of all surfaces.
